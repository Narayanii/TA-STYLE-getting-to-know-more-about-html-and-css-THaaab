Mantra Tea


About Flavours



Mantra Tea is an initiative developed by some friends who desired to add Beauty and Tranquility in this world by the work they do and the way they live.

Once those friends were sitting and enjoying freshly brewed herbal teas in the Himalayan Mount ains . They viewed life on earth as sacred including the teas they loved. From this collective feeling of sacredness emerged the idea of Mantra Tea Since then, these friends have been crafting finest blends of teas from carefully selected, pure & fresh organic herbs.


Hand Crafted

Mantra tea's hand crafted tea bags are made in a sacred atmosphere with continuous chants of Shanti (Peace) Mantra being played with deep intentions of establishing Peace , Love and Harmony in the world. Each tea bag (potli) is made with eco-consciousness using muslin cloth which is pre washed for 24 hours. As a token of our gratitude towards Mother Nature-Mantra Tea's eco-packaging is 100% Earth friendly.
